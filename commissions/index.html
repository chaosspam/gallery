Terms of Service

  1. General terms

    • Prices are subject to change based on demand.
    • I have the right to reject any order for any reason.

  2. Payment

    • Payment is accepted through Ko-Fi or Paypal.
    • Commissions can be paid either 100% upfront or split 50% upfront and 50% after sketch approval.
    • Don't send me any payment before I have agreed to give you a slot and requested you to send me the payment.

  3. Delivery

    • The time it takes to finish your commission varies between 1-2 weeks depending on factors such as health, the complexity of the commissioned piece, and other work. You will get an estimated time after your order is accepted (before you pay).
    • The final deliverable will be
      • A full-resolution image file with no watermark (in PNG format). A smaller image may be requested.
      • A watermarked image file (in PNG format) if applicable.
      • If requested beforehand, a PSD file with the character and background layer separated might be available.

Payment

After the commission is accepted, 50% payment will be requested upfront (can also pay 100% upfront). I will start working on your piece after the payment is processed.

Available payment methods are:

  • Ko-fi
  • Paypal

Your commission may be placed at a lower priority or cancelled if the payment is not recieved within 72 hours of the order.

I will send a rough sketch for approval, if approved, I will request the remaining 50% of the cost before I continue with the piece.

You can ask for the current progress of the piece at any time.

Delivery

When the piece is finished, I can either

  • E-mail you the image as an attachment
  • Send a temporary Google drive link to the image
  • Send the file in a zipped folder through Discord

The final deliverable will be

  • A full-resolution image file with no watermark (in PNG format). A smaller image may be requested.
  • A watermarked image file (in PNG format) if requested.
